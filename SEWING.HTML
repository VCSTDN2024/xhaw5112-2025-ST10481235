Sewing

Stitch your way to independence and creativity. Whether you're looking to start a small business, save money by doing your own alterations, or simply express your creativity through fashion, our Sewing course is a powerful step forward. You'll gain practical skills that open doors to personal growth, financial opportunity, and artistic freedom.

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Cost: R1500
  • Format: Weekly in-person sessions
  • Includes: Hands-on training, mentorship, and certification

Course Content:

  • Types of stitches
  • Threading a sewing machine
  • Sewing buttons, zips, hems and seams
  • Alterations
  • Designing and sewing new garments
