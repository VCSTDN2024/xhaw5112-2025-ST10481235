About Empowering the Nation

Established in 2022, Empowering the Nation is a Johannesburg-based initiative dedicated to upskilling domestic workers and gardeners. Through our two core programs—a six-month Learnership and a six-week Short Skills Training Programme—we’ve helped hundreds of individuals gain marketable skills and unlock new opportunities. Our mission is simple: empower communities through education and practical training.
