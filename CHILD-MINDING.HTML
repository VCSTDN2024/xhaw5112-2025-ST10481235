Child Minding

Caring for the future, one child at a time.

Our Child Minding course is designed to equip caregivers with the essential knowledge and practical skills needed to nurture and support young children. Whether you're a parent, aspiring nanny, or early childhood worker, this course helps you build confidence in creating safe, stimulating, and loving environments for children to grow.

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Cost: R750
  • Format: Weekly in-person sessions
  • Includes: Practical demonstrations, mentorship, and certification

Course Content:

  • Birth to six-month-old baby needs
  • Seven-month to one-year-old needs
  • Toddler care and development
  • Age-appropriate educational toys
  • Creating safe and nurturing environments
