Cooking

Nourish your family, fuel your future.

Our Cooking course is designed to help individuals prepare healthy, affordable, and delicious meals for their families. Whether you're cooking for loved ones or looking to build a foundation for a career in food, this course gives you the confidence and skills to turn everyday ingredients into nourishing experiences.

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Cost: R750
  • Format: Weekly in-person sessions
  • Includes: Hands-on cooking practice, nutritional guidance, and certification

Course Content:

  • Nutritional requirements for a healthy body
  • Types of protein, carbohydrates, and vegetables
  • Planning meals for families
  • Tasty and nutritious recipes
  • Preparation and cooking of meals
