Landscaping

This Landscaping course is designed to equip individuals with the skills needed to provide professional landscaping services for both new and established gardens. Learn how to balance aesthetics and functionality while working with indigenous and exotic plants, fixed structures, and garden layouts.

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Cost: R1500
  • Format: Weekly in-person and online sessions
  • Includes: Practical workshops, mentorship, and certification

Course Content

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed structures (fountains, statues, benches, tables, built-in braai)
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden
  • Aspects of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden layout and design principles
